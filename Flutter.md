| 13               | `*`, `/`, `~/`, `%` | Multiplication, division, truncating division, modulo |
| 12               | `+`, `-`  | Addition, subtraction |
| 11               | `<<`, `>>`, `>>>` | Bitwise shift left, shift right, unsigned shift right |
| 10               | `&`       | Bitwise AND |
| 9                | `^`       | Bitwise XOR |
| 8                | `|`       | Bitwise OR |
